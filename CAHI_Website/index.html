About Clear Advantage Home Inspection


Clear Advantage Home Inspection operates primarily in Western Connecticut and the Hudson Valley areas, and is licensed to operate in both Connecticut and New York states in their entirety.


Bringing more than a half-century of experience in home construction, maintenance and ownership, We explore every detail of your potential new home. Getting a thorough and proper home inspection has the potential to save, or make, thousands of dollars when purchasing or selling a new-to-you home.
